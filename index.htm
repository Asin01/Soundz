How To Get Around

Welcome to Soundz, to listen to music, press the "Soundz" tab on the bottom left. To add and view your favorite songs click on the "Favorites" tab. To watch vides of your favorite artists, visit the Video page by clicking on the "Video" tab. "Tours" tab will provide tour dates and locations. Want to sing-along? Click on the "Lyrics" on the bottom right to see song lyrics. Have questions or comments? You can reach us by click on the mail icon on the top left of this application. If you would like to learn more about us, click on the "i" icon on the top right. You can come back to the home page from any page other than the home page by clicking on the home icon in the footer of this application.
